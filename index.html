About me

Hey! I'm Frederick and I'm a self taught frontend developer based in Los Angeles, CA. I am passionate about creating unique user experiences that are both performant and responsive. I aspire toward a career that will allow me to channel my creativity through creating beautiful software and engaging experiences while continously learning the newest technologies.

As a developer, I'm constantly trying to solve problems and improve upon my current skills/technologies while learning new ones. During my free time I enjoy working on my golf swing, discovering new music, and spending time with loved ones.
